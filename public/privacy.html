LinkedOut Extension Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Today

1. What data we collect

The LinkedOut Chrome Extension only collects the specific text that you manually highlight on a webpage when you click the "Roast Highlighted Text" button. We do not collect personally identifiable information, browsing history, or track your background activity.

2. How we use your data

The highlighted text is sent to our secure backend API and processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate a humorous translation. The original text and the resulting "roast" are safely stored in our database to be displayed on our recent roasts feed.

3. Data sharing

We absolutely do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Data is only transferred to our AI provider (Google Gemini) strictly for the purpose of generating the requested translation.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
